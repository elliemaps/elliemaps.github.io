Camp Randall Commencement Accessibility Map

As a cartographer with the UW Cartography Lab, I had the opportunity to work with the University of Wisconsin-Madison Chancellor's Office and Athletic Department to create some accessibility maps for the spring commencement ceremony that is held in Camp Randall Stadium. The previous maps were a little too visually confusing, so they were looking for a fresh update with a simplified visual hierarchy for ceremony attendees with accessibility needs to use to navigate the large stadium facility.
